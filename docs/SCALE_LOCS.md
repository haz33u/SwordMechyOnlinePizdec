## Why Loc3 jumps to T

Loc2 already ends at **4.75B**. If Loc3 stayed in low B only, it feels like “more Loc2”.  
Putting **T3/T4/Boss in T** makes the suffix ladder **B → T** on the third world, matching “after B comes T”.

combatWall **5T** means: with Loc2 best gear alone you **struggle**; need Loc3 weapons/pets + R4+ and questers.
